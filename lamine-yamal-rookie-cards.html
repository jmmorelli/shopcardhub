Cards to Avoid

Lamine Yamal Cards to Skip in 2026

✗
Unlicensed / sticker-style novelty cards at inflated prices — thin long-term demand vs. Topps Chrome and his earliest Panini issues.
✗
Off-center or surface-worn Chrome raw at full price — a soft-centered refractor is a low grade waiting to happen. Centering is the whole game on Chrome.
✗
Buying the World Cup hype peak — tournament runs spike prices on momentum. If the run cools, raw parallels correct fast. Size momentum buys accordingly.
✗
Raw high-end you plan to grade at market top — grading spread on Chrome is thin once surface/centering is factored. Buy graded or buy clean.
