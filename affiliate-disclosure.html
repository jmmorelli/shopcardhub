The Important Part

Editorial Independence

An affiliate commission never changes a ranking, rating, or verdict. Card values are anchored to recent eBay sold data — period. If a hobby box is a bad value, we rate it a bad value even if we'd earn a commission on the sale. If a prospect is overpriced, the math says so. The commission is on the click, not on the conclusion. Read more about how we price cards.

All card prices on this site are estimates based on recent sold listings as of the date shown on each page. The market moves daily and past sales do not guarantee future prices. Nothing on ShopCardHub is financial advice — it's market data and our read on it. Buy what you can afford to.
