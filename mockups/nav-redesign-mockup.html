NAV Redesign — Mockups

Planning only · not linked · not pushed · noindex

Three directions for re-organizing the nav from a content library (by sport) into a job-based structure that puts the return-visit tools first. Full reasoning in SITE-STRATEGY-REWORK.md. All built in the real site tokens/fonts so the look is honest.

Concept A — Tools-first Recommended now

Collapse 7 sport dropdowns into 4 buckets by job: Tools (retention), Research (SEO guides, deep on click), Shop (money), plus Search + a personal "My Portfolio". Biggest clarity gain, lowest build — it's a config change in nav.json.

Concept B — Dashboard hub Highest ceiling

Minimal nav; the homepage becomes a live dashboard — Signal Board snapshot, your watchlist, top movers. The site is the tool. Most retention upside, most to build.
