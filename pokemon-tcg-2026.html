The biggest Pokémon TCG release of 2026 so far. Greninja is consistently a top-3 most popular Pokémon globally. The SIR Mega Greninja ex ($470–$505) is driving almost all buying energy, with the Hyper Rare Gold version ($330–$485) as a close second. Cinccino is the surprise hit at ~$109, and the Froakie/Frogadier/Greninja Illustration Rare triptych holds ~$80 as a display set.

The Pokemon Center-exclusive ETB (11 packs + stamped Greninja promo) sold out immediately and is now $180–$200 secondary. Sealed booster boxes at ~$307 — buying singles directly is almost certainly better math than cracking boxes at those prices.
Verdict: Buy singles. Boxes at $307 make singles the better EV play on Greninja.
