The #US285 Rookie Debut is the most sought-after Ohtani base rookie card and the one collectors prioritize in PSA 10. It shows Ohtani in his Angels uniform, depicts his debut, and carries the "Rookie Debut" subset designation — a key differentiator from the other 2018 Update variants. This is the card that led in year-over-year appreciation at +61.55%, the strongest growth rate of any major Ohtani base rookie.

With $58M in total eBay Ohtani sales in 2025 and the most searched name on eBay baseball (891,424 active listings), demand for this card is structural — not speculative. Three-time MVP with a World Series ring, playing in Los Angeles, with a two-way skill set that has no comparable in the modern era. The PSA 10 at $494 is historically reasonable given the trajectory.

Grading note: Raw copies of this card have centering and surface issues common to 2018 Topps Update. Pull from a reputable source, inspect carefully, and only submit copies with clean corners and surfaces. The PSA 10 premium (~5–7x raw) makes grading economically compelling on strong copies.
Verdict: Buy — strongest appreciation of any Ohtani base RC. PSA 10 at ~$494 with 62% 12-month growth. The definitive Ohtani rookie card.

The #US1 is the "pitching" Ohtani rookie — him on the mound in his red Angels road jersey. It's the lower-priced entry point into the Ohtani rookie card market at $298 PSA 10 vs $494 for the #US285. Still strong appreciation (+43% in 12 months) and still a legitimate PSA 10 play.

The #US1 appeals to collectors who want the pitching aspect of Ohtani's two-way legacy specifically captured in a card. For those building a complete Ohtani rookie run, this is card #2 after the #US285. For budget-conscious buyers who want Ohtani PSA 10 exposure at a lower price point, this is the logical entry.

The gap between #US285 ($494) and #US1 ($298) has widened over 12 months — the debut card commands a growing premium. This spread may narrow or widen further depending on which narrative — pitching or debut — resonates more with future collectors.
Verdict: Buy for budget Ohtani exposure. PSA 10 at $298 with strong 43% growth. Pitching narrative adds a unique appeal vs. the #US285.

The 2026 Topps Series 1 is Ohtani's first full Dodgers Topps flagship set — he's been in LA since 2024 but 2026 Series 1 represents the most recent flagship issue. The Silver Crackleboard Foil parallel (#200) is among the top 7-day price movers in the Ohtani universe. The diecut numbered auto to /15 cleared $6,500 in eBay sales.

For collectors, the 2026 Topps Series 1 represents an accessible entry point into current-year Ohtani content. Base cards are inexpensive. The play is parallel hunting — Silver Crackleboard, numbered parallels, and any autos you can find at reasonable prices. The Dodgers uniform adds a new visual dimension to collections heavy on the Angels-era cards.

Also watch: The 2019 Topps Prizm #153 Base is among the biggest 7-day movers in the Ohtani market — a secondary card gaining traction with collectors building crossover baseball/Prizm collections.

Cards to Avoid

Ohtani Cards to Skip in 2026

✗
Off-center 2018 Topps Update copies at PSA 10 prices — 2018 Topps Update had centering issues. A raw copy listed at $80 as a "deal" may be a PSA 6 in disguise. Always look at the actual card scan, not just the description.
✗
Non-Topps or unlicensed "tribute" cards — The Ohtani market has a long tail of low-quality third-party and fan-made cards. These have no collector value, no grading potential, and no long-term demand. Always verify year, brand, and card number.
✗
Grading 2018 Topps Update base (non-rookie) cards — Only his rookie-year cards (#US1, #US285, and the Chrome Update parallels) have meaningful PSA 10 premiums. Grading base Ohtani cards from 2019+ without significant parallel premium is a negative-ROI exercise at current grading fees.
✗
Buying on injury or slump news — Ohtani has historically rebounded strongly from market dips. Panic-selling after a bad stretch and then re-buying at a premium is the most common error Ohtani collectors make. Hold through the volatility if your thesis is long-term.
